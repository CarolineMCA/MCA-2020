Comparison of the scores

The automatic transcription from Midi/MuseScore to Audio/Wav and back to Midi/MuseScore resulted in a completely different notation comparing to the file transcribed directly form the score. Its accuracy is also disputable. Just from examining the audio, it is clear the two files differ significantly. The first difference is the lack of one of the three staves in the automatically transcribed file. It is the voice staff that disappeared during the automatic transcription. The lack of the voice part can be heard in the audio file.
Additionally, meter changed from 4/4 to 3/4. This drastically changed the visual notation. The automatically transcribed file is much more complex and has more additional signs around notes than the original. It makes the file very illegible. Just by looking at the two files' output, it is difficult to say they notate the same sound. Furthermore, automatic transcription fastened the whole piece. It sounds much quicker. The tempo changed form moderato (about 88-92 BPM) to a numerical value of 114. The automatically transcribed file also lacks basic metadata visible in the original file.
